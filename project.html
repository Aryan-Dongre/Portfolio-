1. Beauty Parlor Management System

Home

The Home page contains the navigation bar and provides an overview of the main features and highlights of the website.

Service

The services are categorized into four main types: Women, Men, Groom, and Bridal. Users can browse these categories and select the services they want to book.

Appointment

Users can book appointments by selecting services and filling out a form with details such as Name, Email, Phone Number, Date, and Time Slot. An additional service option allows users to add or remove services, and the total price is automatically calculated in real time.

Payment

On the payment page, users can complete their payment using either the same email address used during booking or a different one. After a successful payment, a confirmation email is sent to the provided email address.

Payment success page

The payment success page displays the user's name, email address, and transaction ID as confirmation of the completed payment.

Login

If they do not have an account, they can register for a new one. A password reset option is also available, allowing users to reset their password through a reset link sent to their registered email address.

My Appointment

Users can view their recent and upcoming appointments in this section. It displays complete details including client information, selected services, date, time, payment status, appointment status, and transaction details.

My Appointment History

This section allows users to view their previous appointment bookings. Users also have the option to quickly rebook services from their past appointments.

Admin Dashboard

This is the Admin Panel Dashboard where administrators can view key metrics such as total bookings, services, pending appointments, and available products. It also provides insights into total revenue and the five most recent bookings.

Calendar

The calendar panel allows administrators to view scheduled client appointments by date and service. It includes filtering options to search appointments by client ID or appointment status. Administrators can also analyze monthly booking trends by switching between months.

Service Analytics

Service Analytics helps administrators analyze service performance, identify the most frequently booked categories, and monitor the number of active services available on the platform.

Service Manage

This section allows administrators to manage all available services, including viewing service types, pricing, and status. Admins can add new services, edit existing ones, deactivate services, or remove them from the system.

Booking

The booking management section provides detailed insights into total bookings, paid bookings, pending bookings, and the total revenue generated. A comprehensive table also displays detailed information about each booking.

Revenue

The Revenue section provides detailed insights into business earnings, including which service categories generate the most revenue and monthly revenue trends. It also includes a detailed table showing payment information such as amount, payment status, and transaction details.

Products

This section allows administrators to manage products listed on the website. Admins can view product details such as name, price, brand, weight, and status, and can also add or edit products as needed.
